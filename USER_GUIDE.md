## Common Questions

### Q: Can I edit module names after creation?
**A**: Not currently - name your subject descriptively and use module numbers as references.

### Q: What happens if I close the study streak popup?
**A**: You can click the Study Streak card to reopen it anytime.

### Q: Do custom subjects affect my overall progress?
**A**: Yes! They're included in the calculation for a complete picture.

### Q: Can I add more subjects later?
**A**: Absolutely! Click "Add Custom Subject" anytime.

### Q: Is my data saved automatically?
**A**: Yes! Everything syncs to Firebase in real-time.

### Q: What if I miss a day for the streak?
**A**: Just answer "No" honestly and start fresh tomorrow!

## Troubleshooting

### Progress not updating?
- Check your internet connection

### Study streak popup not appearing?
- Click the Study Streak card manually
- Check that you haven't already checked in today

### Can't add custom subject?
- Ensure subject name is not empty
- Module count must be 1-100
- Try refreshing the page
